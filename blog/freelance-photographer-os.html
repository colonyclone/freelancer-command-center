Core Features of the Freelance Photographer OS

This template is not just a blank canvas; it is a meticulously crafted workflow built on years of freelance photography business best practices. Here is what you get:

1. Comprehensive Client Relationship Management (CRM)

Never lose a lead again. The Clients database allows you to track every inquiry, from initial contact to booked session. Store contact details, communication history, and specific client preferences (like "prefers golden hour lighting" or "has a shy toddler") so you can deliver a highly personalized experience every single time.

2. End-to-End Shoot Management

The Shoots database is the heart of the OS. Each shoot entry includes:

  • Automated Checklists: Pre-shoot prep, contract signing, shot list creation, and post-shoot delivery reminders.
  • Status Tracking: Visual Kanban boards to see at a glance which shoots are in "Inquiry," "Booked," "Editing," or "Delivered" stages.
  • Location & Time Details: All logistical information in one place, easily shareable with assistants or second shooters.

3. Built-in Financial Tracking & Profit Calculation

Knowing your revenue is not enough; you need to know your profit. The Finances database links directly to your shoots, allowing you to log session fees as income and track expenses like travel, props, assistant fees, and software subscriptions. A built-in formula automatically calculates your net profit per shoot, giving you crystal-clear insight into your most lucrative services.

4. Portfolio & Asset Management

Keep your best work organized and ready to pitch. The Portfolio database lets you tag images by category (e.g., Portrait, Wedding, Commercial), mark favorites, and store usage rights information, making it effortless to build targeted pitch decks for future clients.
