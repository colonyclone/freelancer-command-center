Introducing the Freelance Photographer Business OS

The Freelance Photographer Business OS is not just a pretty dashboard; it is a comprehensive, relational database system built within Notion. It is designed to scale with you, whether you are shooting five mini-sessions a month or managing a high-volume wedding photography business.

  1. Client Relationship Management (CRM): Store all client details, communication history, and preferences in one place. Never forget a client's name or their specific vision for a shoot.

How This Template Saves You 10+ Hours a Month
